Blux is wallet infrastructure for Stellar dApps — designed to make onboarding seamless, even for users who don't have a wallet yet.

Users can sign in instantly with **email**, **social accounts** (Google or Apple), **passkeys**, or an existing **wallet**. No friction, no setup, no barriers.
